About

A circle built on trust,
rigour & shared purpose

WISER is an international, independent circle of experienced professional women who combine real-world responsibility with intellectual leadership across sustainability-related fields.

Our members come from academia and professional practice, with expertise spanning sustainable finance, ESG, governance, law, risk, audit, public policy, impact investing, and climate strategy.

The idea for WISER emerged organically from the 2026 Sustainable and Impact Investments International Conference (SIIC) in Hamburg, where we met as peers and recognised the value of creating a trusted, visible, and credible platform together.

Visibility
Amplifying senior women experts in sustainable finance worldwide.
Credibility
Peer exchange, research-informed dialogue, and collective standards.
Influence
Building presence across policy, academia, and financial markets.

WISER is built on

Trust, intellectual integrity, and mutual respect
Long-term ambition with light bureaucracy
Voluntary, flexible, and scalable participation
English as working language, international in scope

Activities

What WISER does

Peer exchange & thought leadership

  • High-level peer exchange across policy, academia, and financial institutions
  • Structured knowledge-sharing at regular virtual meetings
  • Thought leadership posts and statements
  • Participation in international conferences and panels

Research & advisory

  • Bridging professional expertise and academic insight
  • Collaboration across research and practice
  • Positioning members as trusted advisors to organisations
  • Speaking engagements, advisory roles, boards, and high-level committees

Mentoring

  • Internal mentoring among founding and future members
  • External mentoring of the next generation of scholar-practitioners
  • Supporting women combining doctoral studies with professional roles

What WISER is not

  • Not a formal legal entity (at this stage)
  • Not a commercial consultancy firm
  • Not a time-consuming obligation — participation is voluntary and scalable

Membership

A select, invitation-based circle

WISER prioritises quality, relevance, and complementarity of profiles over scale. Membership is designed to ensure a high level of expertise, trust, and intellectual contribution.

Who we look for

  • Senior professional experience in finance, policy, academia, or related fields
  • Doctoral degree (completed or in progress), or equivalent research engagement
  • Cross-sector perspective — spanning disciplines and operational realities
  • International exposure across jurisdictions or regulatory environments
  • Engagement with sustainable finance, governance, or systemic risk

What we value

  • Intellectual rigour and independence of thought
  • Discretion and trustworthiness
  • Willingness to contribute to collective exchange
  • Long-term commitment to sustainability-related issues
  • Trust, integrity, and mutual respect

Selection process

Expression of interest or nomination → Profile review → Internal discussion among founding members → Invitation to join. Membership is not automatic and is reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
